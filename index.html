Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?
We deliver within 3-5 working days across Bangladesh. Dhaka and nearby areas may receive delivery in 2-3 days.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Cash on Delivery (COD), bKash, and Nagad. For bKash and Nagad, you can pay an advance and the rest on delivery.
What is your return policy?
You can return any product within 7 days of delivery if it has a manufacturing defect or is different from what was ordered. Contact us on WhatsApp to initiate a return.
Can I order multiple products at once?
Yes! You can add multiple products and even different variants of the same product using the Duplicate feature in the order form.
Is the fabric quality good?
All KORA ROYAL products use premium quality export-grade fabric. Our shirts and t-shirts are made from 100% combed cotton, and our pants use export-quality fabric for maximum comfort and durability.

Our Policies

Return Policy
